KEN-GR/S6 Integrated Surge Protective Socket

Requirement class to VDE 0675: D
Requirement class to EN 61643-11: Type 3
Requirement class to IEC61643-1: Class III

Application Range:

KEN-GR/S6 series integrated surge protective sockets are applied to the front side of various terminal equipments with AC no more than 250V and rated current no more than 10A, providing protection against overvoltage arising from surge to the power supply line or mis-operation. In combination with signal protection module, they are installed on the front side of a computer network, a telephone, etc., to protect the signal line from overvoltage caused by lightning strike or mis-operation.

Main Features:

1. Adopt three-pin plug and standard signal connector, convenient for installation and replacement.
2. Built-in temperature control and circuit breaking technology and multilayer protection technology, strong protection ability, high reliability.
3.Red light on in notice window indicates normal, off indicates invalid, easy and clear distinguished.
4.Function of multilayer protection and assembly: power, power & RJ45, power & BNC, power & RJ11,etc
5.Independent 2.5mm² grounding terminal, more reliable grounding.

Protection Mode:

A: Surge arrester for Ac power Un:230VAc In:5kA Up<1.5kV
E: Surge arrester for Ethernet (RJ45) Un:5V In:2.5kA Up<45V Vs:100
T: Surge arrester for Telephone (RJ11) Un:110V In:2.5kA Up<350V Vs:40
B: Surge arrester for BNC Un:12V In:5kA Up<80V (Video Signal)
C: Surge arrester for Control signal Un:12V In:5kA Up<80V
V: Surge arrester for TV Un:24V In:5kA Up<80V
F: Surge arrester for Video Un:24V In:5kA Up<80V
