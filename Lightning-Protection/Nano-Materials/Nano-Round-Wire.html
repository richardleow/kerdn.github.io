Nano Round Wire

Product Introduction

Nano corrosion resistant material is the high-efficiency conductive polyurethane coating which is made of nano carbon, polyurethane resin, organic solvents, etc. by special technology and is uniformly coated on forming steel by advanced electrostatic painting technology. The nano conductive coating has high electrical conductivity and high property of resisting acid, alkali and salt corrosion, and can fully prevent the steel core from corrosion.

The product is widely applied to corrosion resistant fields of earthing networks of facilities such as power systems, military equipment and communication base stations and industries such as wireless communication equipment, medical instruments, information processing equipment, aviation and aerospace and home appliances.
