Horizontal Continuous Casting Copper-Clad Ground Round Wire

Product Introduction and Application

Clad steel grounding using high - purity oxygen - free copper by horizontal continuous casting process and quality carbon steel rod, which combined with molecular combination for metallurgy of copper and steel, non - chemical corrosion and galvanic effect.

Continuous casting of copper clad steel grounding lightning protection grounding as a high - tech materials, with the construction difficulty is low, significant advantages such as excellent corrosion resistance strong, conductive, Apply to different geological conditions, especially for coastal, groundwater pollution serious area, with superior price / performance. Horizontal continuous casting of copper clad steel grounding through high efficiency continuous casting technology to achieve high quality low carbon steel and copper core surface realization of metallurgical complex, mutual penetration of metal between the atoms. High electric strength and corrosion resistance of copper and gives grounding with high strength steel material superior long-term security of high - quality ground discharge performance.

Main technical parameter of grounding electrodes
1、copper coating average thickness ≥ 1.0mm
2、Tensile strength ≥ 600Nmm2
3、Straightness error ≤ 1mmm
4、Copper plasticity : grounding rods（wire）Bent at 90 degrees, inside and outside the angular edge crack phenomenon.
5、Copper grade : adhesion test, pointing machine rolling, part deformation phenomenon of copper and steel does not occur with good crack, split.
