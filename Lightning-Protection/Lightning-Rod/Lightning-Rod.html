Product Introduction

First, the principle of protection
When the lightning cloud formation, thundercloud layer will generate a lot of negative charge on the face at the end, thunder cloud and negative strength is generally 7~15kA, thunder cloud of negative charge and the positive charge of the Earth form a powerful magnetic field, Earth positive charge by controllable discharge lightning rod tip relief, and the negative balance of CB cloud the atmosphere, controllable discharge lightning rod continued to absorb and store energy, energy storage to a certain extent by tip for venting into the atmosphere positive charges and negative charge neutralization in CB cloud, Strength of weakened CB cloud of negative charge in advance. To reduce the failure rate, reduce the discharge current, greatly reduced the induced voltage, to reach to avoid and reduce the lightning strength, purpose of protecting the building.

Second, the protection characteristics
1. Shielding aspects of controllable discharge lightning rod has a very large almost against failure of the protected area. For example, when the failure probability is not greater than 0.001(Apparently this under failure probability, possibility of protected objects is tripping is quite small when)Protection angle of up to 55 °, compared with traditional lightning rod actually almost no areas without tripping.

2. Protect range when the protected object failure probability allow achieve 0.1 (point provided for allowed)values，Controllable discharge lightning rod protection angle reached 66.4 °, and the protection of traditional lightning rod protection angle is far below this value (Therefore, in the local application of lightning activity strong traditional lightning protection is more economical, is possibility of lightning protection is also there)
