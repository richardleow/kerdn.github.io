KEN-BOX/PV16-S

SURGE ARRESTER FOR SOLAR & PHOTOVOLTAIC
A solar combiner box is the equipment to combine the DC voltage generated from a number of solar photovoltaic panels. It is an important and necessary equipment in the solar photovoltaic electric generation system, because the electricity and power generated from a single solar panel are too low to get sent to inverter system.
UC of a DC combiner box is 800VDC, in accordance with CE standards,
UC of a DC combiner box is 600VDC, in accordance with UL standards.
