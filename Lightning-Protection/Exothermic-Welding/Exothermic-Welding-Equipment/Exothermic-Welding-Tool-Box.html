Exothermic Welding Tool Box

Product Introduction and Application

During fusion welding construction, special tool should be adopted to adjust the mold holder and the mold, the heat resisting working gloves are used for shielding and the plant material brush is used for cleaning the mold cavity and the joint surface. The tool is put in the RG hot fusion welding tool box.

Model Scope
KEN-TBOX For KEN-WM01, KEN-WM02, KEN-WM03, KEN-WM04, KEN-WM05 Mould
